SlimefunLuckyBlocks adds Lucky Blocks to your server with randomized rewards and events.

## :floppy_disk: Installation

| LuckyBlocks | Slimefun5 | Minecraft |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| UNOFFICIAL  | 5.0.0+    | 1.21.x    |

Place the JAR in your `plugins/` folder. Slimefun5 must be installed first.
